S.O.N.S
Acid Dreams (Jungle Trance Mix)

Besides hailing from South Korea, little is known about S.O.N.S... His music however, speaks for itself. Here we have a dose of mesmeric jungle acid extracted from the second chapter of the Tokyo series: "Shinjuku One Night Stand".

The appropriately named "Acid Dreams" is a trip into the mind's antipodes. Where a heady soundscape is filled with ocean-swirls, squelchy 303s and classic breakbeats. Enough rave for a dance but plenty of depth to simply sit and listen.

Definitely my pick from the double 12" although do check out the rest of the release and Shibuya One Night Stand for more Detroit techno inspired goodies.
