Message In Our Music

SOL1004LP

A collection of heart-breakers, breath-takers and body-shakerz. Waxist's selections of underground modern soul gems from '76 - '83 are packed with shining examples of timeless music from the past. Favourite Records' Sol Discos has delivered a compilation of pure class, each track more potent than the last... an awesome album. A journey back in time that takes you right away. Impossible to pick the album's greatest track, so I thought it would be best to share the lp in its full philharmonic glory.

David Nathan's unreleased version of Ain't Nothing Like The Love is a noteworthy addition to the collection of otherwise previously unearthed jewels. Originally made with John Simmons and Philip Ballou, Bill Toles produced Nathan's version in 1980, using the same backing track and vocals, adding a touch of warmth and blues to the mix.
